Winter

Winter is the coldest season of the year so you have to get creative when looking to have fun. Most of the time you will be stuck in side or at least bundled up when you are out. Fighting off the cold does not mean you have to be lame! You can find many ways to enjoy yourself in the snow.

Some Ideas

From sledding to ice scating, the oppourtunitys in the great out doors are endless. You can find a cold lamp post to stick your toung to or even find an clean patch of snow to eat.

Indoors
